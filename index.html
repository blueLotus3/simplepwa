Welcome to PWA's

What in the world are PWA's(Progressive Web Apps)?

PWA's are web apps that can be installed on your system and can be interacted with offline or without stable internet connection. This benefits users by providing them a smooth and reliable experience when they are navigating on the application.


When building a PWA, a developer's goal is to make the application enticing to be on their home screen as well as reliability no matter their network status. An important tip to keep users enticed is to provide push notifications to keep engagement higher resulting in possible rises in sales.
